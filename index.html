How LeadCove works

Upload. Wait seconds. Start calling.

LeadCove is on-demand. You decide who to reach and when. Upload your CSV, hit run, get back a finished, scored, callable list inside the LeadCove dashboard — typically in seconds. No nightly queue. No CRM required. No copy-pasting between tools.

  1. 01

    Upload your CSV — from MLS or anywhere

    Matrix exports, Paragon, Flexmls, your own spreadsheet, an old prospect list someone gave you, a building canvass you typed up — anything with addresses works. LeadCove is for agents who already have a list to work; if you don't have one yet, build one (canvass a building, export from MLS) and come back.

  2. 02

    We deliver the owner data — name, phone, email

    Whatever's available, we surface — property owner names, mobile and landline numbers, email addresses. Multi-property owners get grouped automatically so it's one prospect per person, not one per building. Our skip-trace partner reports a typical match range of 70–95% depending on list quality + market; a clean MLS export with full residential addresses lands toward the top of that range, a noisy canvass list lower. You only pay for actual matches — no data, no charge.

  3. 03

    We score, clean, and rank

    Every prospect gets a 0–100 score so you know who to call first. Junk rows, duplicates, dead-ends — all stripped. What's left is a ranked list of real people, ready for the dialer.

  4. 04

    Call from the LeadCove dashboard — or your CRM, your choice

    Open Mission Control and start dialing — call, SMS, email, log the outcome, set callbacks, archive passes, all in one screen. Want it in Follow Up Boss instead? Toggle the optional CRM sync (HubSpot, Chime, kvCORE, Salesforce coming) and it lands there too. We're a full prospecting CRM out of the box. Plug into another one only if you want to.

FAQ

Who is LeadCove for?

Licensed real estate agents and brokerages who already have a list to work — solo producers running multi-market campaigns, listing agents working expireds and FSBOs, building canvassers, and brokerages who want every agent on the same prospecting platform. If you sell residential or investment property, you have a CSV of addresses, and you make outbound calls, we built this for you.

How does LeadCove actually find these prospects?

That's our trade secret — and the reason this works. We've built a layered prospecting pipeline that combines multiple legitimate, compliant sources and proprietary logic to give you the most complete contact info at the lowest cost. Every record is tagged with provenance so your team stays squarely TCPA-compliant.

Is this TCPA / DNC compliant?

We don't call or text on your behalf — we deliver prospects to your CRM. You're responsible for DNC scrubs and consent before outreach. Each record carries provenance metadata so your compliance team has what it needs.

I don't have a list yet. Can you still help?

Not today. LeadCove is a contact-enrichment platform — you bring the list (MLS export, building canvass, FSBO list, your own CSV), we deliver the owner data on each match (name, phone, and/or email). If you're brand new and don't have a list yet, build one first: canvass a target building, export from your MLS, ask your brokerage for an old FUB import — anything with property addresses. Then come back.

Do I need a CRM to use LeadCove?

No. LeadCove is a full prospecting CRM out of the box — Mission Control for daily calls, lead manager with phone/SMS/email/notes, pipeline view, archive, analytics. You can run your entire prospecting workflow inside LeadCove without paying for or learning anything else.

What if I already use Follow Up Boss / HubSpot / something else?

Plug it in. Optional one-click CRM sync sends every prospect to Follow Up Boss on day one (HubSpot, Chime, kvCORE, Salesforce coming). Custom fields, stage mapping, and deep-links both ways. Your call — use LeadCove as your CRM, or use it as a prospecting layer that feeds the CRM you already have.

What markets do you cover?

Every county across the state — drop your prospect list and we'll work it wherever the addresses land. Outside our coverage area? Email us and we'll let you know when we're expanding there.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Monthly billing, cancel from your dashboard. Export your enriched leads before you leave — they're yours.

How does pricing work?

Monthly subscription, three tiers: Starter $49/mo (100 credits), Pro $99/mo (300 credits), Power $199/mo (1,000 credits). Same product on every plan — only the included credit volume changes. One credit = one prospect enriched (owner data delivered: name, phone, and/or email). Adding leads to your dashboard doesn't consume credits — only successful enrichment does. Same owner across multiple properties = one credit. No data found = no credit charged. Cancel anytime from your dashboard, credits roll forward. If you ever burn through your monthly allowance, on-demand credit packs are available — but most agents stay within their plan.
