How LeadCove works

Upload. Wait minutes. Start calling.

LeadCove is on-demand. You decide who to reach and when. Upload your CSV, hit run, get back a finished, scored, callable list inside the LeadCove dashboard. No nightly queue. No CRM required. No copy-pasting between tools. From file to phone in minutes.

  1. 01

    Upload your CSV — from MLS or anywhere

    Matrix exports, Paragon, Flexmls, your own spreadsheet, an old prospect list someone gave you, a building canvass you typed up — anything with addresses works. Don't have a list yet? Start free with our newbie path — we'll source one for you.

  2. 02

    We find owners, LLCs, phones, and emails

    Whatever's available, we deliver — owner full names, the people behind LLCs, mobile numbers, email addresses. Multi-property owners get grouped automatically so it's one prospect per person, not one per building. ~95% of the records on a typical upload come back with a verified contact.

  3. 03

    We score, clean, and rank

    Every prospect gets a 0–100 score so you know who to call first. Junk rows, duplicates, dead-ends — all stripped. What's left is a ranked list of real people, ready for the dialer.

  4. 04

    Call from the LeadCove dashboard — or your CRM, your choice

    Open Mission Control and start dialing — call, SMS, email, log the outcome, set callbacks, archive passes, all in one screen. Want it in Follow Up Boss instead? Toggle the optional CRM sync (HubSpot, Chime, kvCORE, Salesforce coming) and it lands there too. We're a full prospecting CRM out of the box. Plug into another one only if you want to.

FAQ

How does LeadCove actually find these prospects?

That's our trade secret — and the reason this works. We've built a layered prospecting pipeline that combines multiple legitimate, compliant sources and proprietary logic to give you the most complete contact info at the lowest cost. Every record is tagged with provenance so your team stays squarely TCPA-compliant.

Is this TCPA / DNC compliant?

We don't call or text on your behalf — we deliver prospects to your CRM. You're responsible for DNC scrubs and consent before outreach. Each record carries provenance metadata so your compliance team has what it needs.

I'm a new agent — I don't have a list. Can you still help?

Yes, that's exactly what the Starter plan is for. Tell us the angle (long-held owners in your zip, absentee landlords, every unit in a specific building, etc.) and we'll source the prospects for you. You don't need to know where to start — we'll get you a callable list on day one.

Do I need a CRM to use LeadCove?

No. LeadCove is a full prospecting CRM out of the box — Mission Control for daily calls, lead manager with phone/SMS/email/notes, pipeline view, archive, analytics. You can run your entire prospecting workflow inside LeadCove without paying for or learning anything else.

What if I already use Follow Up Boss / HubSpot / something else?

Plug it in. Optional one-click CRM sync sends every prospect to Follow Up Boss on day one (HubSpot, Chime, kvCORE, Salesforce coming). Custom fields, stage mapping, and deep-links both ways. Your call — use LeadCove as your CRM, or use it as a prospecting layer that feeds the CRM you already have.
