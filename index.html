How LeadCove works

Upload. Wait minutes. Start calling.

LeadCove is on-demand. You decide who to reach and when. Upload your CSV, hit run, get back a finished, scored, callable list inside the LeadCove dashboard. No nightly queue. No CRM required. No copy-pasting between tools. From file to phone in minutes.

  1. 01

    Upload your CSV — from MLS or anywhere

    Matrix exports, Paragon, Flexmls, your own spreadsheet, an old prospect list someone gave you, a building canvass you typed up — anything with addresses works. LeadCove is for agents who already have a list to work; if you don't have one yet, build one (canvass a building, export from MLS) and come back.

  2. 02

    We surface owners, phones, and emails

    Whatever's available, we surface — property owner names, mobile and landline numbers, email addresses. Multi-property owners get grouped automatically so it's one prospect per person, not one per building. In our Florida beta to date, ~95% of records come back with phone and/or email matched. That number is live and will move with new usage; match rates vary by list and market.

  3. 03

    We score, clean, and rank

    Every prospect gets a 0–100 score so you know who to call first. Junk rows, duplicates, dead-ends — all stripped. What's left is a ranked list of real people, ready for the dialer.

  4. 04

    Call from the LeadCove dashboard — or your CRM, your choice

    Open Mission Control and start dialing — call, SMS, email, log the outcome, set callbacks, archive passes, all in one screen. Want it in Follow Up Boss instead? Toggle the optional CRM sync (HubSpot, Chime, kvCORE, Salesforce coming) and it lands there too. We're a full prospecting CRM out of the box. Plug into another one only if you want to.

Features

Everything a real estate agent actually needs.

Live, real-time lookups

Every enrichment is a 1–3 second API call — not a stale list, not a pre-cached file. The phone and email you see is what's available right now.

Owner contacts surfaced

Property owner phone and/or email surfaced on every record we can match. Records we can't match cost you nothing — no data, no charge.

Same-owner = one credit

One owner with ten units? You pay once — phone and email auto-propagate to all ten properties. Multi-property landlords are pure margin.

Lead scoring

0–100 score on every prospect — equity, years held, ownership type, contact completeness. Priority A on top, hold leads at the bottom. Call the best first.

Auto-cleaning

Junk rows, government-owned, HOAs, PO boxes, duplicates — anything that's not a real prospect, gone before delivery. We don't waste your credits on bad rows.

Bring your own list

LeadCove starts with the list you already have. Drop in a CSV — MLS export, building canvass, FSBO scrape, your own spreadsheet — anything with property addresses. We surface the contacts; you make the calls.

Full CRM included

Mission Control, lead manager, pipeline, archive, analytics, global search — work prospects end-to-end without ever leaving LeadCove. CRM sync (Follow Up Boss day one) is optional, not required.

Call outcome logging

Tap one button after hanging up. Status updates everywhere automatically — connected, voicemail, callback, declined, all logged with timestamp.

Archive workflow

"Not interested" passes go to an archive, not the trash — restore anytime if circumstances change.

Credit-based pricing

1 credit = 1 prospect enriched. Adding leads doesn't consume credits. Misses don't consume credits. Same owner across multiple properties = 1 credit total. Credits never expire while your account is active.

Provenance tracked

Every record carries metadata about where it came from and when. You're still responsible for DNC scrubs and consent before outreach — your compliance team has what they need, the rest is your call.

Export anywhere

Your prospects, your CSV. No lock-in. No hostage-taking. Your data leaves with you the moment you ask for it.

FAQ

Who is LeadCove for?

Licensed real estate agents and brokerages who already have a list to work — solo producers running multi-market campaigns, listing agents working expireds and FSBOs, building canvassers, and brokerages who want every agent on the same prospecting platform. If you sell residential or investment property, you have a CSV of addresses, and you make outbound calls, we built this for you.

How does LeadCove actually find these prospects?

That's our trade secret — and the reason this works. We've built a layered prospecting pipeline that combines multiple legitimate, compliant sources and proprietary logic to give you the most complete contact info at the lowest cost. Every record is tagged with provenance so your team stays squarely TCPA-compliant.

Is this TCPA / DNC compliant?

We don't call or text on your behalf — we deliver prospects to your CRM. You're responsible for DNC scrubs and consent before outreach. Each record carries provenance metadata so your compliance team has what it needs.

I don't have a list yet. Can you still help?

Not today. LeadCove is a contact-enrichment platform — you bring the list (MLS export, building canvass, FSBO list, your own CSV), we surface the owner phone and email. If you're brand new and don't have a list yet, build one first: canvass a target building, export from your MLS, ask your brokerage for an old FUB import — anything with property addresses. Then come back.

Do I need a CRM to use LeadCove?

No. LeadCove is a full prospecting CRM out of the box — Mission Control for daily calls, lead manager with phone/SMS/email/notes, pipeline view, archive, analytics. You can run your entire prospecting workflow inside LeadCove without paying for or learning anything else.

What if I already use Follow Up Boss / HubSpot / something else?

Plug it in. Optional one-click CRM sync sends every prospect to Follow Up Boss on day one (HubSpot, Chime, kvCORE, Salesforce coming). Custom fields, stage mapping, and deep-links both ways. Your call — use LeadCove as your CRM, or use it as a prospecting layer that feeds the CRM you already have.

What markets do you cover?

All 67 Florida counties — every market in the state, live May 2026. From Pensacola to Key West, Jacksonville to Naples, drop your prospect list and we'll work it. Outside Florida? Add your city to the waitlist and we'll tell you when we're expanding there.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Monthly billing, cancel from your dashboard. Export your enriched leads before you leave — they're yours.

How does pricing work?

Monthly subscription, three tiers: Starter $49/mo (100 credits), Pro $99/mo (300 credits), Power $199/mo (1,000 credits). Same product on every plan — only the included credit volume changes. One credit = one prospect enriched (phone and/or email surfaced). Adding leads to your dashboard doesn't consume credits — only successful enrichment does. Same owner across multiple properties = one credit. No data found = no credit charged. Cancel anytime from your dashboard, credits roll forward. If you ever burn through your monthly allowance, on-demand credit packs are available — but most agents stay within their plan.
