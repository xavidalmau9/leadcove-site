How LeadCove works

Four moves. Zero manual steps.

  1. 01

    Tell us who you want to reach

    Expired listings. Withdrawn. FSBOs. High-equity owners held 10+ years. Absentee landlords. Every unit in a specific building. Sellers in a zip code with a canceled listing in the last 90 days. Pick from our ready-made filters or describe your own — we'll set it up. Then point us at your CRM: Follow Up Boss at launch, HubSpot, Chime, kvCORE, Salesforce coming.

  2. 02

    We enrich overnight — free first, paid second

    Owner names come from public property records (free). Phone and email from a layered pipeline: property-appraiser sites, LLC officer records, then paid skip-trace only if everything else fails. We group by owner so you never pay twice for the same person.

  3. 03

    We score and filter

    Equity, years held, rental flag, sale propensity, absentee-owner signal, property type — stacked into a 0-100 score. Government, HOA, and placeholder rows are auto-excluded. Duplicates are collapsed. What's left is what you actually want to call.

  4. 04

    Priority A leads land in your CRM — every morning or on demand

    Hot Prospects stage in Follow Up Boss (or HubSpot, Chime, kvCORE, Salesforce, etc.). Custom fields for score, equity, years held, list price, MLS number. Run an on-demand pull anytime a new MLS export drops. Deep-link back to the LeadCove dashboard for full context. You start calling. We stay out of the way.

FAQ

Where does the owner data come from?

Free public-records first (county property appraiser, state business registry). Paid skip-trace (BatchData) only when free options fail. Every phone and email is tagged with its source so your team stays TCPA-compliant.

Is this TCPA / DNC compliant?

We don't call or text on your behalf — we give you the data. You're responsible for DNC scrubs and consent before outreach. We surface the data-source label on every record so your compliance team has what it needs.

Which CRMs are supported at launch?

Follow Up Boss on day one. HubSpot, Chime, and kvCORE on the roadmap — request any CRM on the trial signup and we prioritize based on customer demand.

What markets do you cover?

Launching with Miami-Dade (Matrix MLS). Broward, Palm Beach, and Orange County (Orlando) rolling out next. Public-records access is state-by-state — drop us a note if your market is different and we'll tell you our ETA.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Monthly billing, cancel from your dashboard. Export your enriched leads before you leave — they're yours.

What happens during the free trial?

25 enriched leads, full access to every feature, your CRM connected end-to-end. No card required. If you like it, pick a plan. If you don't, we delete your data.
