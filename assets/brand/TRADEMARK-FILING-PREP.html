6. Brand context (background for counsel)

Industry: Real estate technology (proptech). The product helps licensed real estate agents and brokerages identify property owners and obtain their contact information for outbound prospecting.

Geographic scope: Currently launching statewide in Florida (May 2026). Federal registration is appropriate; state registration in Florida may be added later if counsel recommends.

Mark composition:

  • "Lead" + "Cove" — coined / suggestive composition. "Lead" is descriptive of the prospects/leads central to the service; "Cove" suggests a coastal/Florida origin and a place where leads gather.
  • The visual mark uses two horizontal wave glyphs and a sun, reinforcing the coastal theme.
  • Color palette is navy and teal; type system uses Fraunces (serif) and Inter (sans-serif).

Pre-existing trademark search: A formal USPTO TESS search has not yet been conducted by counsel. We are unaware of conflicting marks but rely on counsel for the clearance opinion. Notable considerations:

  • "Lead" is heavily used in tech/SaaS naming; descriptive use is common.
  • "Cove" appears in unrelated industries (e.g. "Cove Smart Home," "Cove Software"). None known in real-estate-prospecting space.
  • The compound "LeadCove" appears unique in our preliminary searches.
