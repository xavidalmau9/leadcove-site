What LeadCove is

LeadCove is a property-owner data and lookup tool built for working real estate agents. Users drop in an address (or a list of addresses) and get back the verified owner's name, every phone number with DNC + TCPA-litigator flags, every email address, and the registered officer when the property is held by an LLC. The single-screen workflow replaces the multi-site lookup most agents stitch together across TruePeopleSearch, Spokeo, BeenVerified, and similar consumer-records sites.

What problem it solves

Most cold real estate prospecting workflows currently require an agent to switch between four to seven separate websites to resolve a single property address into a usable contact record — owner name, phone, email, DNC status, and the human behind an LLC. The average agent spends two to three hours per week doing this manually for a 40-row weekly list. LeadCove compresses that work into a single dashboard with one credit charged per successful match (no charge on misses).

Compliance posture

LeadCove flags every phone number against the national Do Not Call registry and surfaces known TCPA litigators inline. State-level Do Not Call lists (Florida, Indiana, Louisiana, Missouri, Pennsylvania, Tennessee, Wyoming) are also cross-referenced where applicable. Owner contact data is sourced from licensed B2B providers and public property records; the platform does not aggregate private data outside what's legally available to professional users.
